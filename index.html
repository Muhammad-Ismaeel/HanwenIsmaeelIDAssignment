Tourist Map for COVID-19

This site has relevant information for frequently visited countries and regions by Singaporeans.

By clicking on the country you are visiting, you will be brought to the Ministry of Foreign Affair's guidelines when visiting that specific country

The MFA has particular guidelines to follow due to the COVID-19 virus so that you, as a tourist, can stay safe.

Good Practices to follow when visiting countries with high COVID-19 cases:

Be up-to-date with healthcare

Make sure you are protected with the latest vaccinations so that you are more immune to the virus, reducing the chances of you catching the virus. Having the latest vaccine keeps your immunity higher.

Choose lodging carefully

It is important to understand the safety procedures the lodging has in place to accommodate the COVID-19 pandemic and how they are helping protect all invloved. Key elements to look for include the cleaning procedures, enforced social distancing and contactless payment options.

Maintain good hygeine

Wear masks or face coverings, wash your hands diligently, disinfect surfaces, and maintain social distance. While travelling, be prepared and bring your own PPE, hand sanitiser, and cleaning supplies to disinfect any highly touched areas.
